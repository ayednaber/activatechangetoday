I'm Sophie Ashley - Hypnotherapist, Health & Nutrition Coach

I am originally from the United Kingdom where I was a police officer for 10 years. I emigrated to Canada in 2003. I became interested in hypnotherapy when I became fearful of flying. I received a couple of EFT sessions (Emotional Freedom technique, commonly known as tapping) and a hypnotherapy session. After 3 sessions, I got back on a plane after swearing I would never fly again. That particular flight was surreal and encouraged me to train in EFT and hypnotherapy.
