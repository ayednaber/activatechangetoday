I'm Sophie Ashley - Hypnotherapist, Health & Nutrition Coach

After 3 sessions, I got back on a plane after swearing I would never fly again. That particular flight was surreal and encouraged me to train in EFT and RTT. RTT changed my life it empowered me to be the best version of myself.
